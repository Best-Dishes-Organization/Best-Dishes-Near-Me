Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Overview

Best Dishes is designed to help you discover top‑rated dishes nearby or worldwide while respecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Location (optional): If you grant permission, we use your approximate device location to show dishes near you. You can disable this in Settings and still browse worldwide.
  • App Usage: We may collect basic, aggregated analytics (e.g., feature usage, performance) to improve the app. No third‑party tracking is used.
  • User Content (future updates): If you choose to contribute reviews or photos in future versions, that content will be stored securely in iCloud (CloudKit).

How We Use Information

  • Provide core functionality (e.g., location‑based results).
  • Improve performance and reliability.
  • Support customer service and troubleshooting.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share personal data with third‑party advertisers. When enabled, user content is stored in Apple’s iCloud (CloudKit) and may be publicly visible within the app.

Your Choices

  • You can disable location access at any time in iOS Settings.
  • You can use the app without creating an account.
  • When contribution features are available, you may be able to delete your content and/or account in‑app.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at bestdishesnearme@gmail.com.
