Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Overview

Best Dishes is designed to help you discover top‑rated dishes nearby or worldwide while respecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Location (optional): If you grant permission, we use your approximate and precise device location to show dishes near you. You can disable or configure this in Settings and still browse worldwide.
  • App Usage: We do not currently collect personal analytics or tracking data. We may collect basic, aggregated, non-identifying usage data in the future to improve the app.
  • User Content: If you submit reviews or photos, that content is stored in iCloud (CloudKit) and visible to other app users.
  • Account: Sign in with Apple provides a user identifier to associate your reviews with your account.

How We Use Information

  • Provide core functionality (e.g., location‑based results).
  • Improve performance and reliability.
  • Support customer service and troubleshooting.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share personal data with third‑party advertisers. User content is stored in Apple’s iCloud (CloudKit) and is visible within the app.

Your Choices

  • You can disable location access at any time in iOS Settings.
  • You can browse without an account; posting requires Sign in with Apple.
  • You can delete your reviews in‑app.

Moderation and Safety

  • We review reports and may hide or remove content that violates our Terms.
  • We may block abusive users or restrict posting for policy violations.
  • Moderation actions are logged in CloudKit for auditing.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at bestdishesnearme@gmail.com.
